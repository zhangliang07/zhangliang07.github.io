Why did I decide to apply for Ph.D. student?

I always have a dream to become a teacher in college. I wish to take part in research works because I am interested to explore unknown fields and create new methods in my major. 
In the future, I hope I could help students to improve their ability of learning and research, especially those slow students. I have many similar experiences with them and could help them more.

In the days of school, I once got some chances to get enrolled in doctoral courses, but I missed them. After commuting in companies for many years, the dream is not fade off. I made up my mind to apply an overseas Ph.D. degree.
After I graduated with Ph.D., I plan to go back to China to become a teacher in a professional college or a regular college.
